🌿 Our Background

Born from a
real need.

Renew started with a simple question: why is it so hard to actually remember what we read? Our founders — a small group of friends who met in a Bible study — kept forgetting the very verses that had moved them most.

So they built something. Not a flashcard app, not another notetaking tool — but a gentle, daily companion that meets you where you are and helps one verse at a time sink in deep.

Renew is designed to feel as natural and nourishing as watering a plant — small acts, done faithfully, that lead to something beautiful.

🌱 Our History

How we got here.

2022

The Idea 💡

Three friends in a Bible study realize there's no app that actually helps verses stick. The first sketches for Renew appear on a whiteboard.

2023

Building the Team 🌿

Developers, a designer, and a theology grad join the mission. The core learning engine and verse library are built from scratch.

2024

Beta Testing 🧪

400+ beta testers across churches and small groups put Renew through its paces. Their feedback reshapes the app entirely for the better.

2025

Refinement 🎨

Every screen, animation, and interaction is polished until it feels effortless. Accessibility, dark mode, and daily reminder features added.

2026
