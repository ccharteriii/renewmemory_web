Why you should download

Renew utilizes proven active recall techniques tailored for scripture memorization. Instead of passively reading, you interactively fill in the blanks, solidifying the verses in your long-term memory. Our bubbly, distraction-free interface makes learning a daily habit you actually look forward to.

Why you should trust us

Built by a passionate team of developers and designers, Renew is engineered for performance and privacy. We believe that spiritual tools should feature top-tier, app-native design. Your progress is stored securely, and our methods are rooted in cognitive science.
